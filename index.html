About

Seattle has many things to offer, which is why many people reside or visit this wonderful city. What Seattle has to offer also brings in tremendous traffic. Seattle's traffic is busier compared to other cities. While exploring transportation datasets from the Seattle GeoData , Accessible Pedestrian Signals and Traffic Cameras interested me the most. As a UW student, I wanted to see the locations of Accessible Pedestrian Signals (APS) and traffic cameras around UW campus.
