Accessible Pedestrian Signals (APS)

The data shows the locations of existing Accessible Pedestrian Signals (APS) within the city of Seattle. APS are devices that use non-visual formats such as auditory signals or vibrational for tactile signals. These non-visual formats **efficently communicate** the information of when it is safe to walk or not at intersections. The aim is to provide these features to support pedestrians who are blind or have low vision.
